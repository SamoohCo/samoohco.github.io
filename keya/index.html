Keya Vadgama

Type Designer & Brand · Building at Plumb

Keya is a designer at the intersection of typography, brand, culture, and product who specializes in typefaces that support South Asian scripts and custom logotypes. She recently released her first font, Mango, her love letter to the Gujarati script. She previously worked on product and design at Plumb.
