About Samooh

A network built on
trust and craft.

We are building a community for founders, makers, and builders of Indian origin who are working on exciting world-class products and apps for a global audience.

As we see an increase in the number of global startups and apps coming out of the Indian diaspora, we also feel the need for a sense of community among us. We want to gather the brightest minds working on things that have a big impact everywhere — including India.

Samooh is an initiative to provide makers with a supportive network of people who have been there, done that, and are happy to pay it forward.
